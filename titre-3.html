Ask for support and coaching

  • Personalized coaching in activity design, UX and interaction design
  • Introduction to the methodologies of scientific research and creative practices (from product design to artistic creation) to strengthen your storytelling skills and your ability to make original propositions
  • Integrating the Studio's “learning community”: making contact with users from the school, the IPP, the plateau or even outside
  • Feeding that community: organizing events, meetings, group projects, workshops, hackathons, etc.
  • Introduction to “systemic approaches” that enable to take into account the needs and outcomes of our creations, from every possible angle
  • Using the Studio's technical resources (presented in the following section)
