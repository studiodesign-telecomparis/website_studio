Quantitative UX

Teacher(s)

Panos Mavros, Stéphane Safin

Goals

Using the methods and tools of quantitative user-experience research to understand how users interact with an interactive system, and what can be improved regarding specific needs and goals

Institution and classes

IGD (IPP), Ingénieur (Telecom), Recherche en design (ENS)

Number of students

?

Duration

24h

Theoretical and practical grounds

UX design, UI design, user studies, user research, quantitative research methods

Knowledge to acquire

Fundamentals of quantitative UX research

Skills to develop

Practices of UX and UI Design. Designing and running data collection and analysis, including competitive analysis, feature audit and analysis, interviews / surveys / questionnaires, usability and accessibility testing, task performance measurement, satisfaction / sentiment / preferences measurement analysis, reviews collection and analysis, A/B testing, user journey, journey map, weak and strong points analysis, activity tracking and recording, quantitative data visualization and analysis, personas

Presentation of some productions

  • SynapseS (pedagogy management app): Easier access
  • Food Delivery App
