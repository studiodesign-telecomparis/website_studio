Overview

The Studio Design in Telecom Paris is a bright, modular space open to all students and researchers, dedicated to creation, innovation and collaboration.

Together, we design, prototype and test our innovative ideas. We discover a variety of approaches and know-how, from creative, research or business practices. We meet students and teachers from various disciplines and institutions. We take advantage of the high visibility provided in that ecosystem. We develop new skills or acquire new knowledge. We share sources of inspiration, and explore personal questions with others !

Go to the "Use" page to get more info, or to the "Discover" page to see projects.

 

Who is it for ? Why is it useful for me ? 

Think design and user experience right from the conception of your creations, in order to make them useful, adapted and effective.

Born entrepreneurs and creatives of all kinds, from business to digital arts through software and hardware design and development, you are all welcome to the Studio Design ! Whether it is for a single moment of sharing or for regular work sessions on a long-term project.
